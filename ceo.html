Biography & Expertise

Marcus Zhee Lee Hong is a recognized pioneer in algorithmic trading and machine learning, holding advanced degrees in Computational Finance and Artificial Intelligence from MIT. Prior to founding ZHC Corp, he spent a decade leading quantitative research teams at leading hedge funds, developing low-latency execution systems that manage billions in assets.

His vision for ZHC Corp was born from the conviction that sophisticated institutional-grade AI trading tools should be accessible to the everyday user. He focuses heavily on the ethical deployment of AI, emphasizing risk management and user-centric security design above all else.
